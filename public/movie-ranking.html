The Best Movies According to Kat

My top 3 movies of all time

Tank Girl

Lori Petty gives the comic book heroine life in this super fun action packed movie with a killer soundtrack.


Interview with the Vampire

As a huge fan of Anne Rice novels and New Orleans, this has always been one of my fave films.


Paul

One of the first movies I worked on in the vfx industry, Paul is a comic masterpiece bought to life - an RV trip in the US with a loveable alien along for the ride, what could be better?!
